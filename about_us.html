The purpose of this system is to facilitate record keeping. The technology aided in the long-term storage and retrieval of theses from both undergraduate and graduate levels. The basic ideas of this system revolve on the broader context of record information management, electronic record management, and other facets of information technology. The records can be managed and kept  up-to-date with the help of this system. 

The consistency of the electronic-based document processing system is going to be improved as a result of the new archive management system that has been proposed. Additionally, it has the potential to reduce the amount of time needed to accurately scan the papers.

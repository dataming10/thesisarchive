The Digital Thesis Archiving Tool was successfully introduced in Taguig City University's CICT System Fair 2022. The Digital Thesis Archiving Tool came in fourth position, and it had been first since entering the top five for system presentation. The consistency of the electronic-based document processing system is going to be improved as a result of the new archive management system that has been proposed. Additionally, it has the potential to reduce the amount of time needed to accurately scan the papers. 

In order to learn more about the system and its contents, students can use the Student Module. Students need only sign up using their existing login information. After registering for the system, the administration will check to make sure the student is who they say they are before allowing the student to submit the project for review. The user has the option of revising the previously submitted project. One can also include his or her own works, whether or not they were ever made public, in the list of submissions.  The purpose of this system is to facilitate record keeping. The technology aided in the long-term storage and retrieval of theses from both undergraduate and graduate levels. The records can be managed and kept  up-to-date with the help of this system.

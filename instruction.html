Introduction

The Digital Thesis Archives is created for those student that who wanted to share their thesis from others. The purpose of having this technology is to help other reseachers to look for an ideas, reference, and inspiration from their own work and research. It is used for:

  • Serving reference, ideas, and knowledge
  • Have a credibility for reseachers work
  • Helps the institution for better future
  • Inspire other users and viewers

What can Digital Thesis Archives do?

  • Generate lesser time in scanning their records.
  • Life cycle of documents.
  • It can easily search the thesis record of the students.
  • Provide easy access of information such as the title, name of the group and the date of submission..

Why Digital Thesis Archives?

The proposed archive management system is in charge of improving the consistency of the electronic-based document process system. It can also reduce the accuracy of time when scanning papers.

Submitting Thesis

Students of Taguig City University is the only person that can upload a thesis.

However, archiving a thesis has it's limitation. This is for the students only. Other users of Digital Archives has it's own instruction.

The thesis should already be hardbound and handed over to the thesis librarian.

To prevent mislead and fraud documents, the thesis librarian is the responsible for checking and verifying the thesis.

Only the title page, abstract, and table of contents are permitted to be archived.

This because the main idea of having Digital Thesis Archives is only for the users to have an ideas and reference. To also prevent the copying the content of the students and steal credits from them.

Data Act Privacy

Republic Act No. 10173, otherwise known as the Data Privacy Act is a law that seeks to protect all forms of information, be it private, personal, or sensitive. It is meant to cover both natural and juridical persons involved in the processing of personal information.

The content should not be plagiarized.

Users is allowed to view and download the thesis. Again, archiving the thesis has it's limitation. meaning to say, viewers can only view the abstract, title page, and table of contents of the thesis.

Users will be responsible for accessing the thesis and downloading them.
